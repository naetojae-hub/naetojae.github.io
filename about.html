About

I'm Naetoja Ebere Ezerioha, a data analyst in training with a growing toolkit in Excel, SQL, Power BI, and Python. Outside of coursework, I'm focused on building projects that mirror real business questions — the kind an employer would actually ask an analyst to dig into.
